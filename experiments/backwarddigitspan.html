[Executive Function & Memory] (Working & Short-term Memory) Backward Digit Span

In the backward digit span task, smaller models have decreased accuracy after 15-20 digit (mean backward digit span of human is 5). Like humans, LLMs find the reversal operation makes this task more difficult.

  • Mean model accuracy, by digit sequence length, in the backward digit span task. Larger models do better at the backward digit task than their smaller counterparts.
  • Perplexity of the correct sequences in the backward digit span task yields more nuance, e.g., note that Qwen2-7B accuracy is equal for lengths 15, 20, and 30, the perplexities indicate that its understanding of 30-length digits is worse.
